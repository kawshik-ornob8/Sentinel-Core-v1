About Me

Cyber Security Expert & AI Researcher

I am Kawshik Ahmed Ornob, a tech enthusiast and I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering from Daffodil International University (DIU). I bridge the gap between robust security protocols and intelligent systems.

Through this platform and my Kawshik360 blog at blog.kawshik.dev, I aim to simplify complex technological concepts for a global audience. From decoding SIM card anatomy to discussing the future of AI-driven 'Vibe Coding' and sharing my journey "From Junior to Pro: 5 Habits That Actually Made Me a Better Developer," my mission is to provide researched and actionable insights for developers alike.
